Clipnyx is a lightweight clipboard history manager that lives in your macOS menu bar.

It automatically records copied text, images, URLs, source code, colors, and more — ready to recall whenever you need them.

KEY FEATURES
- Instantly open the history panel with a hotkey (⌘⇧V)
- Select and paste directly with number keys (1–9)
- Auto-categorizes 11 content types: text, images, PDF, URLs, HTML, CSV, source code, and more
- Category filters and text search to quickly find what you need
- Pastes directly at the cursor position in the source app
- Thumbnail previews for images and PDFs
- Store up to 500 history items (choose from 20/50/100/200/500)
- Customize which categories to record
- Fully customizable hotkey
- Launch at login support
- History is stored locally. Nothing is ever sent externally.
